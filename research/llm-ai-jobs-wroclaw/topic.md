# Topic Contract: llm-ai-jobs-wroclaw

## Goal

Dwa razy dziennie przygotowywac aktualny, zrodlowany research o nowych i
najciekawszych ofertach pracy przy modelach LLM i praktycznych systemach AI,
z naciskiem na Wroclaw oraz zdalna prace z Polski.

## Scope

- Include: Wroclaw onsite/hybrid roles, remote Poland roles, machine learning
  engineer, LLM engineer, NLP engineer, applied scientist, RAG, AI agents,
  AI platform/infrastructure, MLOps for generative AI, model evaluation,
  inference, fine-tuning and closely related hands-on engineering roles.
- Exclude: login-only listings, unsourced reposts, generic data/BI roles
  without meaningful AI or LLM work, pure sales or marketing roles, unpaid
  internships, scams, speculative headhunter posts without employer or source,
  and legal/financial advice about employment.

## Priority

High

## Keywords

- Primary: LLM engineer Wroclaw, AI engineer Wroclaw, machine learning engineer
  Wroclaw, NLP engineer Wroclaw, generative AI Poland remote, RAG engineer
  Poland, AI agents engineer, applied scientist Poland, MLOps generative AI
- Secondary: Wroclaw hybrid AI, remote Poland LLM, ML platform engineer Poland,
  inference engineer, fine-tuning engineer, model evaluation engineer, vector
  search, retrieval augmented generation, Python ML, PyTorch, Transformers

## Source Policy

- Use current web research on every run because job listings change quickly.
- Prefer public job boards and public company career pages.
- Do not require login to LinkedIn, social media, or private communities.
- Record source links for every material claim and every listed opportunity.
- Treat public job board snippets as leads; prefer canonical employer career
  pages when available.

## Report When

- A new relevant role appears.
- A previously seen role has a material change: salary, location, remote policy,
  employer, title, seniority, stack, deadline, status, or canonical URL.
- A high-signal employer in Wroclaw or remote Poland opens, closes, or changes
  a relevant AI/LLM hiring page.
- No material change; then write a short `No material change` report with
  checked sources and no repeated role list.
